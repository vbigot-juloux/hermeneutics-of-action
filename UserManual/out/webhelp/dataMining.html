Data mining

Note: XML-TEI elements: teiCorpus|TEI|text|group|text|body|div
Data mining is divided in two sections:
  1. Within transcription: (a) primary data, and (b) objective variables which principal aim is to count verb occurrences and their TAXO, subTAXO according to AEs.
  2. Interpretation: (a) primary data, (b) objective variables, and (c) subjective variables for the interpretation of AE's action.

Explanation through a practical example: passage KTU 1.3:ii:4b-16 of the Cycle of Baʿlu and ʿAnatu.
