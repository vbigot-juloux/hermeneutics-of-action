Text mining

Note: XML-TEI elements: teiCorpus|TEI|text|group|text|body|div[n]
Text mining is divided into two sections:
  1. Within transcription: (a) primary data, and (b) objective variables which principal aim is to count verb occurrences and their TAXO (category), subTAXO (subCategory) according to animated entities.
  2. Interpretation: (a) primary data, (b) objective variables, and (c) subjective variables for the interpretation of animated entities' action.

Explanation through a practical example: passage KTU 1.3:ii:4b-30 of the Cycle of Baʿlu and ʿAnatu.
